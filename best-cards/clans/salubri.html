Salubri

The Salubri and their antitribu are a rare sight in tournaments. This could change following the Lost Kindred expansion release in 2018. They have unique disciplines, respectively b and l. For now, they are seen in mainly two archetypes: Saulot & Friends, a wall deck featuring Saulot, The Wanderer, and Spirit Marionette, using b and the eponymous Spirit Marionette.

Highlights

Repulsion (33 decks)

A good stealth modifier that can become permanent. An excellent way for Salubri to bump up their game as they do not have access to many other stealth enhancer given their clan disciplines.

Spirit Marionette (29 decks)

An iconic card of the b discipline, it has brought up an archetype of its own. Stealing an opponent minion is always good, the central issue is to abuse, cripple or destroy it afterwards.

Gift of Sleep (24 decks)

A second stealth enhancer, still at no cost, a good card for B.

Saulot, The Wanderer (23 decks)

Star of the Salubri clan, Saulot has both B and L at superior, along a nice set of disciplines. His abilities are also impressive.

Renewed Vigor (22 decks)

Bringing back any big capacity vampire to full capacity is often a good way to bloat, or even turn a game around. This is the only card of the game allowing this along Giant's Blood.

Anesthetic Touch (20 decks)

An excellent combat ends at superior B, it is a bit weak at inferior b.

Sense Death (18 decks)

An excellent rush action, with high maneuverability and a most desirable +1 stealth.

Neutral Guard (13 decks)

A good block denial at superior B for a big capacity vampire like Saulot, The Wanderer.
