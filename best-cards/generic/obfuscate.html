o Obfuscate

o provides the best stealth in the game, as its name suggests.

Lost in Crowds

played in 484 decks, typically 2-6 copies

The best stealth money can buy, played in almost all o constructs.

Elder Impersonation

played in 419 decks, typically 2-4 copies

This block denial modifier comes in very handy against a single minion. It works against all minions and vampires and can bypass even high levels of stacked intercept.

Faceless Night

played in 406 decks, typically 2-4 copies

A single unlocked minion can be hard to get around if multiple actions are in order. This is an excellent tool in this case as it lets you lock the defending minion on the first block failure. Furthermore, it will work as a block denial against allies.

Swallowed by the Night

played in 382 decks, typically 1-5 copies

A versatile stealth modifier, the maneuver option comes in handy in decks with a decent combat module. Others find the combat option handy to avoid jamming the hand with unneeded stealth.

Cloak the Gathering

played in 329 decks, typically 1-5 copies

An additional source of stealth that becomes an excellent tool when not all vampires have o. It can also be stacked, by the vampire doing the action himself, then by another vampire with O.

Spying Mission

played in 255 decks, typically 1-4 copies

An additional stealth modifier that can be stacked, it is also a decent counter against bounces: it allows one to suppress a high damage bleed that has been bounced to its grand-prey, which is actually a common situation.

Forgotten Labyrinth

played in 238 decks, typically 2-5 copies

Especially useful in decks with a political module, it gives such a high bonus to undirected actions that they become virtually unblockable. Its uselessness for directed actions still makes it absent from a lot of o modules.

Veil the Legions (193 decks)

played in 200 decks, typically 1-4 copies

Less widely used than Cloak the Gathering because it offers no direct stealth on an action, its efficiency increases in decks with low capacity vampires: a vampire with O can give +1 stealth to multiple consecutive bleeds, a nice setup for a lunge move.
