Nosferatu

The Nosferatu and their antitribu use the oip disciplines. Some good vampires of theirs are used in multi-clan crypts, like Nananimalism or The Dark Side of Politics. They are seen on their own in only one prominent archetype: the Nosferatu Royalty, a toolbox using Camarilla Princes and Justicars.

Highlights

Stick (80 decks)

The cheapest I pool can buy, he gets included in many i decks.

Beetleman (71 decks)

A cheap I minion, he also gets included in many i decks.

Petra (40 decks)

5 pool is a bit pricey for I so she is less seen in mono I or multi-clan decks. Her ability can be useful against The unnamed or Cybele though.

The Labyrinth (35 decks)

One pool for permanent stealth is really a bargain: other clans have to pay 2 pool for this kind of device. Only the Followers of Set have a similar tool with Opium Den.

Warsaw Station (28 decks)

A rare effect, quite useful in Nosferatu decks using political actions, like the Nosferatu Royalty archetype.

Gustaphe Brunnelle (25 decks)

Appreciated for his rush ability, he is often included in combat-oriented PI decks like the Guruhi Rush archetype.

Nikolaus Vermeulen (25 decks)

The iconic vampire for Nosferatu Royalty decks, he has an excellent ability that helps get other Princes out faster and cheaper, or bloat a bit.

Calebros, The Martyr (23 decks)

A Prince available for a really cheap price, even though his ability can be a problem depending on the context.

Alonso Petrodon (22 decks)

His title and bleed bonus, combined with the rare DO discipline combination, makes him a good pick in some political decks, like in the Mistress archetype.

Murat (21 decks)

A Prince for the very reasonable price of 7 pool, he is often seen in Nosferatu Royalty decks alongside Nikolaus Vermeulen and Calebros, The Martyr.

Selma the Repugnant (16 decks)

Yet another Prince, she is a bit expensive and does not even have superior I. Still, she can find her place in Royalty constructs using O, like in The Dark Side of Politics archetype.

Beast (15 decks)

Always included for his ability and strength combined with Pc, he gets included in P decks like for example in the Euro Brujah archetype. He is often seen alongside Theo Bell, as they share similar abilities.
