Gangrel

The Gangrel and their unique j discipline are very versatile and provided many different archetypes. Some of the most successful were the Stanislava archetype and related Camarilla Royalty builds. Another one is Renegade Assault, using Renegade Garou as main minion.

Highlights

Earth Meld (126 decks)

It is considered the best « combat ends » card of the game. It comes at no cost at inferior j, plus the unlock option at superior J, still at no cost.

Form of Mist (113 decks)

An excellent combination of « combat ends » and stealth, one of the most versatile tool of j.

Earth Control (85 decks)

2 stealth at superior J is a strong stealth modifier, totally worth 1 blood. Combined with Form of Mist, this makes Gangrel and their antitribu almost as stealthy as any vampire with o.

Claws of the Dead (67 decks)

The most dreaded combat card of the j discipline, it is used after strikes have been chosen by both vampires, before damage resolution, and never wasted against a dodge or a combat ends. The maneuver at superior J can come in handy. Finally, it works with a hand strike so Immortal Grapple has no use against it.

Bobby Lemon (63 decks)

An excellent disciplines combination for just 4 pool, he gets even included in mono i decks.

Homunculus (63 decks)

An excellent disciplines combination for just 4 pool, he gets even included in mono i decks.

Aksinya Daclau (56 decks)

Star of the Gangrel antitribu, her ability is second to none. She gave birth, alongside Cybele, to the Girls Will Find Inner Circle archetype.

Rapid Change (53 decks)

Another stealth and « combat ends » combination. The cost of 1 blood is a bit pricey, but it completes the stealth arsenal of j along Form of Mist and Earth Control.

Ecoterrorists (51 decks)

A goof bloat device, generating 1 pool per turn, it can also help get vampires into play a bit faster early game.

Céleste Lamontagne (49 decks)

A nice discipline combination for just 5 pool, she gets a place even in mono i decks.

Backways (44 decks)

Even if it is a little bit pricey, the permanent stealth effect is a good one, moreover if the construct relies on j for stealth.

Instantaneous Transformation (40 decks)

An powerful and versatile stealth / unlock combination, mostly available to Gangrel antitribu, but also to some rare vampires with access to the JC combination.

Ingrid Rossler (38 decks)

A star among Gangrel, the fact that she has d and is a Prince is a nice combination. Moreover, her bonus in influence can be a very nice edge in the early game.

Stanislava (32 decks)

Star Inner Circle of the Gangrel Clan, Stanislava not only has D but also C, giving her access to Instantaneous Transformation alongside Govern the Unaligned and Deflection. Her ability against allies also is quite a nice advantage against constructs using them along The Unmasking.

Enkidu, The Noah (32 decks)

The most dreaded Gangrel antitribu, he has such a powerful disciplines and abilities for combat he finds his place in many rush archetypes.

Janey Pickman (30 decks)

A good disciplines combination and a very threatening combat ability, she makes a nice addition in many combat-oriented decks.
