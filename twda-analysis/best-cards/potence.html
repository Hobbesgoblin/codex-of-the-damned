p Potence

p provides an excellent combat module.

Immortal Grapple (152 decks)

One of the few combat cards that can counter combat ends, it also prevents the use of weapons and dodges. At P, it even gives a press, which can be useful offensively as well as defensively.

Disarm (101 decks)

Given the damage potential of p, it can be used after a round of combat more often than not. This is a good tool to disable a cumbersome vampire. When combined with c additional strikes or f prevention, it is even easier to play.

Torn Signpost (98 decks)

The bread and butter of a majority of p combat modules, it provides +2 damage at no cost and works well with additional strikes as well as presses.

Preternatural Strength (63 decks)

A nice alternative to Torn Signpost when one want to save some card slots in a deck.

Heroic Might (63 decks)

A stronger but more expansive version of Preternatural Strength. Depending on the deck, one or the other might be better.

Iron Glare (45 decks)

A bleed modifier available to PR decks. It is better than Aire of Elation for non-Toreador decks, as it does not cost a blood. The alternate use for some votes at inferior can come in handy if the deck has some political actions.

Slam (43 decks)

A good strike by itself, the maneuver is useful for pure p modules that do not have better options for maneuvers.

Thrown Sewer Lid (36 decks)

An alternative against long range combat modules instead of trying to get to close range. The optional press at superior P can even close the range for a second round. Not too many copies can be included as it can jam the hand.
