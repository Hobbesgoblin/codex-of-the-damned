Highlights

This is a rush deck using Deep Song to rush or bleed and a i combat module with Carrion Crows and Aid from Bats.

Using Nana Buruku allow to play a lot of Ashur Tablets for a nice recursion. This let the deck adapt itself to the table context all the while bloating a bit.

Fame and Dragonbound are used to improve pool damage a bit.

Using low ad mid capacity vampires let the deck use a decent amout of naked "bleed for 1" actions or inferior i Deep Song to get some pool damage to its prey.

It is a very solid deck, with good defece against the predator and decent pool damage towards its prey, although it is not a very fast damage dealer, rush actions let it control the table nicely.

Tips & Tricks

Powerbase: Luanda and Well-Marked are nice tools for a rush deck, although they can be used by both Nana Buruku and Fish.

The deck does not have a lot of intercept, but plays Powerbase: Montreal and The Rack nonetheless, relying on the threat of a retaliation rush to protect them.

Just including a bit of wakes and some intercept enhancers like Cats' Guidance and Sense the Savage Way keep the other players guessing on wether the deck can block or not.

Getting Wider View at some point helps getting rid of the spurious Nana Buruku in crypt. She's also only played in 4 copies to avoid jamming the crypt too much; other minions are needed for the deck to work well.

Variants

A modern variant of this deck uses Anarch Convert to make the vampires anarch, along Anarch Revolt to improve pool damage, taking down any Anarch vampire on the prey side using rush actions. Orian Gissler's deck from 2018 is a good example.

Weenie i is a strong build anyhow and can even be played without Nana Buruku and Ashur Tablets. Kim Nilsson's deck from 2016 is a good example.

Some builds include more intercept, like Ádám Horváth's deck from 2010, using Aranthebes, The Immortal and Raven Spy.

Errata

Pentex™ Subversion has been modified by an errata in September 2019. It does not prevent a vampire to act anymore, so it is less powerful and should probably not be included in the deck anymore.
