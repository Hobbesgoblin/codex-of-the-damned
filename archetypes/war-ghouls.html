Highlights

This is a rush deck using multiple War Ghoul to rush the opponents vampires.

The idea is to get a War Ghoul into play as soon as possible, using a low capacity Tzimisce and Jake Washington.

The War Ghoul then proceeds to taking down every vampire on the table, using Fake Out and Trap as needed.

Other allies and retainers can be recruited to be sacrificed to more War Ghoul. Ossian, if he is drawn, may even be better than a War Ghoul.

The deck uses low capacity vampires with A for bleed bounce and intercept. Pool damage is done with Dragonbound and naked bleed once all the prey's vampires are down.

Tips & Tricks

This a fast and aggressive deck and will likely attract the table attention. It should try to keep control of the whole table if possible.

Letting some vampires live on the predator side can be a good way to bounce their bleed and deliver additional pool damage to the prey.

Variants

There is a variation using Maris streck for Parity Shift and Anathema. Check Emiliano Imeroni's deck from 2012

There is also an anarch version using Anarch Convert and Piper in order to bring War Ghoul into play unopposed. Check Antonio Cobo Cuenca's deck from 2008
