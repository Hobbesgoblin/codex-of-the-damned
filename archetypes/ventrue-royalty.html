Highlights

This is a toolbox deck using Ventrue Princes.

The idea is to get the highest capacity vampire out, then use Govern the Unaligned at superior D to get more vampires out fast and cheap, then later to bleed with the help of some d modifiers.

The intercept module is built around the fact the many vampires in the crypt are Princes and uses Second Tradition: Domain heavily. Deflection is used to bounce bleed in case they get thtough anyway.

The combat module uses f for damage prevention and Weighted Walking Stick to deal more damage.

Tips & Tricks

Daring the Dawn and Foreshadowing Destruction are used for the lunge. The use of Direct Intervention on a modifier like Conditioning prevents it to be played again on the same action (as a modifier can only be played once and, even if countered it has been played). Using an alternative bleed enhancer is OK though, so Foreshadowing Destruction can even save a lunge this way.

Scourge of the Enochians and Anarchist Uprising are counters against breed constructs, as they can become overwhelming for this deck.

Variants

There are many variations to this archetype. Parity Shift and Entrancement are often included, Seduction can help to get the bleed through. On the Master side, Papillon and Island of Yiaros are common inclusions too. Finally, some Freak Drive to increase ABC is often seen too.

It is also possible to ditch the combat module and replace it with some Majesty, with a good number of Freak Drive for multiple actions. With more damaging votes like Kine Resources Contested, it makes for many aggressive actions, and they quickly become hard to block. This is the Law Firm variant. Anthony Coleman's deck from 2009 is a good example. It is also often played with a group 1-2 crypt, as Emerson Bridges and Sir Walter Nash are good Princes for the job, at a low cost. Sergio Gracia's deck from 2009 is a good example.

Errata

Pentex™ Subversion has been modified by an errata in September 2019. It does not prevent a vampire to act anymore, so it is less powerful and should maybe not be included in the deck anymore. Still its ability to disable a strong blocker is useful for the lunge, moreover in the Law Firm variant.
