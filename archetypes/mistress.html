Highlights

This is a stealth politics & bleed deck starring Mistress Fanchon. Her ability let her fetch an Alastor to equip with an Helicopter paying only 1 pool.

The Villein, Zillah's Valley and Information Highway is used to get multiple big capacity vampires into play.

This version uses only the DO disciplines, a dreadful combination. Obedience and Mental Maze are used for combat defence.

Tips & Tricks

The key idea of this archetype is to abuse Mistress Fanchon's ability to fetch the right card at the right time. The focus of the deck is to make sure she can do that and bleed every turn.

Scourge of the Enochians is included to cripple breed constructs, as they can be overwhelming for this slower deck.

Variants

The Alastor + Helicopter combination is not mandatory. Other methods can be found to improve ABC, for example using Flurry of Action like in Benoît Moyen's deck from 2010.

Danilo Torrisi played a nice combination in 2018: The Might of the Camarilla alongside Possession to get more Inner Circle into play fast and cheap.

Although this is a different archetype, Mistress Fanchon can be used for her AV combination, alongside Malgorzata, Sha‑Ennu or Unmada for a variation of the Tzimisce Wall archetype.

Errata

Pentex™ Subversion has been modified by an errata in September 2019. It does not prevent a vampire to act anymore, so it is less powerful and should maybe not be included in the deck anymore. Still its ability to disable a strong blocker is useful for the lunge.
