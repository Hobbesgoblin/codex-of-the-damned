Highlights

Often spelled "!Ventrue Grinder", (the "!" prefix has been used to denot the antitribu clans for a while), this is a toolbox deck using the Ventrue antitribu clan and their fad disciplines combination.

The idea is to get the highest capacity vampire out, then use Govern the Unaligned at superior D to get more vampires out fast and cheap, then later to bleed with the help of Conditioning and Bonding.

The intercept module uses A for intercept, wakes and bounces, and d for additional bounces with Deflection.

The combat module uses f for damage prevention and Weighted Walking Stick to deal more damage.

Tips & Tricks

Daring the Dawn is kept for the lunge.

Variants

There are many variations to this archetype. The Coven is a rare inclusion, as the deck is supposed to grind the blood out of the table, giving opponents the opportunity to get some of it back is a bit of a stretch. The Rack is more often seen. The Kiss of Ra, Misdirection and Seduction can be used to help get the bleed through. Abbot, Bowl of Convergence and permanent intercept locations like KRCG News Radio are often included to help with intercept. Ivory Bow and Target Vitals are often seen in the combat module, while Freak Drive is sometimes added to increase ABC.

The combat module can also use Concealed Weapon and guns, like in Ravel Zorzal's deck from 2016.

There are some alternatives on the crypt side: For example, Lazverinus, Thrall of Lambach and Dylan can be used for j in order to include Claws of the Dead and Earth Meld, like in Jackson Iribarren's deck, winning SAC in 2009.

Bob Joseph's deck won the NAC in 2008 with a variation focused on bleed, using only Aura Reading with some f prevention for the combat module.

Errata

Pentex™ Subversion has been modified by an errata in September 2019. It does not prevent a vampire to act anymore, so it is less powerful and should maybe not be included in the deck anymore.
