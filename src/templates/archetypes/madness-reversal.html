Madness Reversal

{% endblock %} {% block archetype %}>

Highlights

This is a special deck using {{ card("Madness Network") }} and {{ card("Reversal of Fortunes") }}.

The idea is to get some low capacity Malkavians out fast together with a {{ card("Madness Network") }}, then play {{ card("Reversal of Fortunes") }} during the prey's turn to play again, then again on the predator's turn to play again, etc. playing twice as much as the opponents.

The first vampires get a bit locked in the combination, and the rest of the deck focuses on playing a lot of Master cards, {{ card("Brainwash") }} and {{ card("Kaymakli Nightmares") }} to prevent the opponents of even getting a vampire out.

The deck then kills with simple bleeds and {{ card("Smiling Jack, The Anarch") }}.

Tips & Tricks

Rotation is tricky in this deck, and {{ card("Dreams of the Sphinx") }}, {{ card("Specialization") }} and {{ card("Heart of Nizchetus") }} all help in this regard.

Although {{ card("Victor Pelletier") }} is not a Malkavian, his ability is priceless in this deck, as {{ card("Reversal of Fortunes") }} needs some votes to get through.

Variants

There are not many variants of this deck. {{ link("/deck-search", _("Orian Gissler's deck"), twda_id="2k9battlefrenzypf") }} from 2009 was the original attempt.
